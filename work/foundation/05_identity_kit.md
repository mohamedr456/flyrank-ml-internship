## Type

- **Headings:** Space Grotesk (free, Google Fonts)
- **Body:** Inter (free, Google Fonts)

## Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Main | Deep teal | `#0F766E` |
| Text | Near-black | `#111827` |
| Background | Warm near-white | `#FAFAF9` |
| Accent (max one) | Amber | `#D97706` |

## Two-line style note (pasted into my Claude Project as a standing instruction)

> Space Grotesk headings, Inter body; near-black on warm white with deep teal `#0F766E`
> and at most one amber `#D97706` accent.
> Mood in one sentence: calm and technical — numbers first, so the work is the loudest
> thing on the page.
